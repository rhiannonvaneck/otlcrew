This styleguide can be used to review the styles of this website.

With a little thought, a website can be created with good typography alone. Some commentators have come straight out and said that good webdesign is 95% typography.

Establish a nice Measure

The measure is essentially the line length.

Some Typographers say setting your measure to three alphabets long is a good guide.

Maybe do that now by setting max-width: 40ems; in your css body selector.

Possibly add ligatures for a little class

This will require you select a decent font that includes some ligature glyphs.

A fiend be they who waffle and design a future absent of ligatures.
