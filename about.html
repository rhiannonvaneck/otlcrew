Six wild lads from South Australia have joined forces to bring you great entertainment. The talented riders are just looking to have some fun flogging their dirt bikes.

Their off road riding test their skills. Canyons, fallen trees and dirt mounds are forever shaping and defining them as riders. With their lack of fear, they always find mad ways to avoid natures obstacles.

The OTL Crew spend their spare time riding their dirt bikes. Each of the lads compete in endurance dirt bike races across Australia. When they are not racing they have a passion for mucking around on country properties.
